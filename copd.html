COPD & Asthma Management

Resources for Better Respiratory Health

Comprehensive information and resources for managing COPD (Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease) and asthma. Learn about treatment options, equipment, and lifestyle strategies for better breathing. Both conditions are chronic respiratory diseases that require ongoing management and proper equipment for optimal quality of life.

COPD is a progressive lung disease that makes it difficult to breathe, typically caused by long-term exposure to irritating gases or particulate matter. Asthma is a condition in which airways narrow and swell, producing extra mucus and making breathing difficult. Both conditions benefit from proper medication management, respiratory therapy, and supportive equipment.

Our respiratory therapy specialists provide expert guidance on nebulizer therapy, oxygen concentrators, and other essential equipment to help you manage your respiratory condition effectively. We offer comprehensive education on proper inhaler techniques, breathing exercises, and environmental modifications that can improve your symptoms and quality of life.

Management strategies include medication adherence, avoiding triggers, regular monitoring with peak flow meters, pulmonary rehabilitation, oxygen therapy when prescribed, and having an action plan for exacerbations. Our team works closely with your pulmonologist to ensure you have the right equipment and knowledge to manage your condition successfully.
