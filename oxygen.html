Oxygen Therapy

Home Oxygen Concentrators & Support

Our oxygen therapy services provide reliable home oxygen concentrators and portable oxygen solutions to help patients maintain their independence and quality of life. Oxygen therapy is essential for patients with chronic respiratory conditions, helping to improve energy levels, reduce shortness of breath, and enhance overall well-being.

We offer complete oxygen therapy support including equipment delivery, patient training, and 24/7 technical assistance throughout the Des Plaines area and Chicago suburbs. Our licensed respiratory therapist ensures proper equipment setup, flow rate adjustments, and safety training for both patients and caregivers.

Home oxygen therapy involves the use of oxygen concentrators, which extract oxygen from room air and deliver it at prescribed flow rates through nasal cannulas or face masks. This therapy is commonly prescribed for patients with COPD, pulmonary fibrosis, heart failure, and other conditions that affect oxygen levels in the blood.

Our oxygen services include comprehensive equipment assessment, installation and setup, patient education on proper use and safety, regular maintenance and cleaning, insurance authorization assistance, and emergency backup support. We coordinate with your physician to ensure your oxygen therapy meets all clinical requirements and optimizes your health outcomes.
